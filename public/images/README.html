Option 1: Use Your Own Images

Place your images in public/images/ with the following names:

  • agency-workspace.jpg - Cluttered workspace photo (reportage style)
  • expertise-vitrines.jpg - Product/storefront photo
  • expertise-ecommerce.jpg - Lifestyle photo with mobile
  • expertise-saas.jpg - 3D render or isometric mockup
  • portfolio-1.jpg through portfolio-6.jpg - Project mockups
  • team-group.jpg - Group photo
  • team-1.jpg through team-5.jpg - Individual portraits
  • team-1-hobby.jpg through team-5-hobby.jpg - Hobby photos

Option 2: Use Placeholder Services

You can use free placeholder image services like:

  • Unsplash - Free high-quality photos
  • Lorem Picsum - Random beautiful photos
  • Placeholder.com - Simple color placeholders

Option 3: Generate Colored Placeholders (Quick Start)

The site will work fine without images. CSS gradients will show in their place.

Once your site is running, you can add images incrementally.
